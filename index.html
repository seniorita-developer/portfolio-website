Dolphin Game - My first Pygame experience

Inspired by legendary Alien Invasion! Dolphin Game is a two-dimensional shooter game in which the player controls a dolphin by moving him horizontally and vertically across the screen and firing at incoming fishers` boats. The goal is to defeat all fishers` boats that move towards the dolphin. The more boats are defeated, the higher their speed is. Once you defeat all the boats, the next wave comes up. When a boat collides with the dolphin, he loses one of his five lives. Help the dolphin to protect his territory from the agressive fishers!

  • Code repository: Seniorita Developer
  • Date: July 2017
  • Category: Mini-game
